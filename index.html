About BrainStorm

BrainStorm Notes Club is a student-run platform dedicated to making quality study materials accessible to everyone. We believe that learning is better together.

Our mission is simple: create a space where students can share their notes, help each other understand difficult concepts, and succeed academically as a community.

Whether you're catching up on missed classes, preparing for exams, or just want to see different perspectives on a topic, BrainStorm has you covered.
